Kids Class

Level 1: SNAPPER 1

For children who are new to swimming, hesitant about water, or have little to no swimming experience.

Main Goal: To be relaxed and comfortable in the water.

Skill to be achieved:
- Comfortable in the water
- Relax on assisted backfloat
- Comfortable with water poured over face
- Kicks on tummy with assistance

Level 2: SNAPPER 2

For children ready to swim without a parent’s assistance, developing basic survival swim techniques.

Main Goal: To swim independently for short distances while building basic water safety skills.

Skill to be achieved:
- 10m Survival Swim
- 10m Dog Paddle
- Floating independently for a few seconds
- Basic water entry and exit

Level 3: JAMMER 1

For children developing longer swim distances with a focus on freestyle and survival swimming.

Main Goal: To increase swimming distance and improve stroke technique, with focus on freestyle and survival swim skills.

Skill to be achieved:
- 15m Survival Swim
- 15m Basic Freestyle
- Improving breath control while swimming
- Kicking with proper technique

Level 4: JAMMER 2

For children learning advanced strokes, focusing on backstroke and butterfly.

Main Goal: To refine technique in advanced strokes and develop water coordination and balance.

Skill to be achieved:
- 15m Backstroke
- 15m Butterfly
- Introduction to proper stroke techniques
- Body coordination and balance in the water

Level 5: JAMMER 3

For children refining their technique in advanced strokes, preparing for pre-competitive swimming.

Main Goal: To perfect swimming techniques for efficiency and endurance, with an introduction to competitive swim skills.

Skill to be achieved:
- 15m Breaststroke
- Advanced Freestyle mechanics
- Stroke efficiency and endurance
- Learning basic turns and transitions
